Programs

Strength Training

Our strength training program includes free weights, resistance machines, and bodyweight exercises. Trainers customize routines for all fitness levels, ensuring proper form and progression. You'll learn techniques to safely lift heavier and improve overall muscle endurance.

Cardio Fitness

Cardio Fitness combines HIIT, treadmill, cycling, and dance-based workouts. Classes are designed to increase stamina, burn calories, and improve cardiovascular health. Suitable for beginners and advanced participants who want to challenge themselves.

Yoga

Yoga classes focus on breathing, alignment, and meditation techniques. Different styles including Hatha, Vinyasa, and Yin are offered. Enhance balance, posture, and mental clarity in a welcoming and supportive environment.

High Heels Dance

This program teaches elegance, rhythm, and self-expression through high heels choreography. Classes include warm-up, technique drills, and full dance routines. Perfect for all skill levels looking to improve posture, strength, and dance confidence.
